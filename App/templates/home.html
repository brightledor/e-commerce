About Eatery Express

Welcome to Eatery Express, your local destination for authentic Ghanaian and Nigerian dishes made with love, tradition, and flavor! We serve hearty home-style meals that bring people together — from the rich aroma of jollof rice and egusi soup to the comforting taste of banku, eba, and fufu with spicy stews. At Eatery Express, we celebrate the best of West African cuisine — fresh ingredients, bold spices, and recipes passed down through generations. Whether you’re craving a quick lunch, a family meal, or a taste of home, our kitchen delivers fast, fresh, and flavorful food that satisfies every bite. Come and experience the warmth, culture, and soul of Africa — all in one plate. Eatery Express: Fast. Fresh. Truly African. Learn More About Our Journey
